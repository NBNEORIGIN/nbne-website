Precision Acrylic Cutting Service

We cut acrylic (perspex) sheets to your exact measurements for replacement greenhouse panels, display cases, protective screens, machine guards and DIY projects. Supply your dimensions and we'll CNC-cut clear or coloured acrylic to size.

All panels are cut on our CNC router for accurate, clean edges. We work with standard sheet acrylic in various thicknesses and can supply clear, frosted, or coloured options.
