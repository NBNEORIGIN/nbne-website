Industrial Metal Tags & Serialised Asset Labels

Batch production of laser-engraved metal tags with QR codes, barcodes and sequential numbering. Manufactured in Alnwick for UK-wide supply.

Batch Production of Industrial Metal Tags

NBNE manufactures metal tags and asset labels for facilities managers, engineers and manufacturers. We handle batch runs from 100 to 10,000+ tags with laser engraving, QR codes, barcodes and sequential numbering.

Tags are CNC-cut from aluminium, stainless steel, brass or copper. We work from your data (CSV or spreadsheet) to encode unique information on each tag. Typical applications include asset tracking, machinery labels, valve tags and donor recognition.

Manufactured in-house at our Alnwick workshop with UK-wide delivery.

Materials & Finishes

We manufacture metal tags from a range of durable materials suited to different applications:

Aluminium

Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, ideal for indoor and outdoor use. Most popular choice for asset labels.

Stainless Steel

Heavy-duty, highly durable, suitable for extreme environments and high-wear applications.

Brass

Premium finish with natural patina over time. Perfect for heritage, marine, and decorative tags.

Copper

Antimicrobial properties, distinctive appearance, ideal for specialist industrial applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

We can produce small batches from as few as 10 tags, but pricing becomes more competitive at higher volumes (100+, 500+, 1,000+). Contact us with your requirements for a tailored quote.

How long does production take?

Standard turnaround is 5-7 working days for most orders. Urgent production is available for an additional fee. Large batches (5,000+) may require 10-14 days.

Can you match our existing tag design?

Yes. Send us a photo or sample of your current tags and we can replicate the design, dimensions, and finish. We can also improve durability or add new features like QR codes.

Do you offer design services?

Absolutely. Our in-house design team can create professional tag layouts, incorporate your branding, and optimise for readability and durability. Design service is included for larger orders.

What fixing options are available?

We offer pre-drilled holes (with or without countersinking), adhesive backing (permanent or removable), cable tie slots, and rivet supply. Let us know your preferred fixing method when requesting a quote.

Can tags be supplied with sequential numbering?

Yes. We can laser engrave or UV print sequential numbers, alphanumeric codes, or custom serialisation formats. This is ideal for asset tracking and inventory management.

Are your metal tags suitable for outdoor use?

Yes. Aluminium and stainless steel tags are highly weather-resistant. Laser engraving provides permanent marking that won't fade in sunlight or wash off in rain. UV printing can be laminated for additional protection if required.
