Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 2-3 weeks from the initial consultation. Timeline depends on how quickly you can provide content and feedback.

Do I need to provide the content?

Yes, you know your business best! You provide the text, images, and information. We can help guide you on what content works well, but the core content comes from you.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! We'll show you how to make simple text and image changes. For more complex updates, we're here to help during your support period.

What about domain names?

If you already have a domain, we'll use that. If not, we can help you register one (domain registration costs around ¬£10-15/year, paid directly to the registrar).

Yes! We use Netlify's free tier which is perfect for business websites. It includes fast global CDN, SSL, and form handling. No hidden costs.

What happens after the support period ends?
