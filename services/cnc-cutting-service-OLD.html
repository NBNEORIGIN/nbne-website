Professional CNC Cutting in Northumberland

NBNE Print & Sign operates advanced CNC routing equipment at our Alnwick facility, providing precision cutting services for commercial clients across Northumberland and the North East. Our CNC cutting service delivers accurate, repeatable results for signage manufacturing, display fabrication, and custom panel cutting.

Whether you need a single prototype or a production run of hundreds, our in-house CNC capability ensures fast turnaround, competitive pricing, and consistent quality. We work with a wide range of materials and can cut to your exact specifications from supplied artwork or technical drawings.

Based in Alnwick, we serve businesses in Morpeth, Amble, Rothbury, Ashington, Newcastle, and throughout the UK with collection and delivery options available.

Materials We Cut

Our CNC router can process a wide variety of sheet materials:

Aluminium Composite (ACM/Dibond)

Lightweight, rigid panels ideal for outdoor signage and displays. Weather-resistant and easy to fix.

Acrylic (Perspex)

Clear or coloured acrylic sheets for illuminated signs, displays, and protective screens.

MDF & Plywood

Timber-based boards for interior signage, exhibition stands, and structural components.

Foamex (PVC Foam Board)

Lightweight, cost-effective material for temporary signage and indoor displays.

Correx (Corrugated Plastic)

Durable, waterproof boards for estate agent signs, site boards, and temporary signage.

Solid Aluminium

Heavy-duty metal cutting for structural signage, brackets, and industrial components.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We work with vector files (AI, EPS, PDF, DXF, DWG) and can also work from technical drawings or sketches. If you're unsure, send us what you have and we'll advise.

What is the maximum cutting size?

Our CNC bed size is 3m x 2m, which covers most signage and display applications. For larger projects, we can cut panels in sections and provide joining details.

How long does CNC cutting take?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 working days from artwork approval. Urgent jobs can often be accommodated‚Äîcontact us to discuss your deadline.

Can you supply the material as well?

Yes, we stock a range of commonly used materials and can source specialist materials to order. Material supply is included in our quotes.

Do you offer finishing services?

Absolutely. We can print, laminate, paint, polish, or apply vinyl graphics to your cut panels. We also offer installation services for signage projects.
